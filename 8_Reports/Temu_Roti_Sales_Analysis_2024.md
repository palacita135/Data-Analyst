# Temu Roti Annual Sales Report (2024)

**Period:** January 1, 2024 - December 31, 2024
**Total Revenue:** Rp48,499,809.00
**Total Transactions:** 2,160

## 1. Executive Summary

Temu Roti experienced a significant sales surge in mid-2024, with **June** being the standout month driving 59% of the annual revenue. The business shows a clear preference for weekend shopping, with **Sunday** being the peak sales day.

## 2. Detailed Performance Analysis

### A. Daily Analysis (Best Days)

**Sunday is the peak sales day**, averaging Rp24,718 per day.

- **Sunday**: Best Performing Day
- **Wednesday**: Strong mid-week performance (Rp23,270).
- **Friday**: Lowest sales day (Rp20,260).
  _Insight: Customers likely stock up on Sundays for the week or treat themselves on weekends._

### B. Monthly & Quarterly Performance

The year was defined by a massive spike in **June (Q2)**.

- **Q1 (Jan-Mar)**: Rp5.7M (Steady start)
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun)**: **Rp33.4M** (Dominant quarter, driven by June sales of Rp28.6M)
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep)**: Rp6.5M (Stabilized)
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec)**: Rp2.7M (Lowest quarter)

### C. Half-Yearly Review

- **H1 (Jan-Jun)**: **Rp39.2M** (81% of total revenue)
- **H2 (Jul-Dec)**: Rp9.3M (19% of total revenue)
  _Critical Finding: Revenue is heavily front-loaded in the first half of the year._

### D. Top Products (by Volume)

1.  **Es Kopi Susu**: 194 units (Best Seller)
2.  **Roti Cranberry 2 pcs**: 177 units
3.  **Roti Cranberry**: 165 units
4.  **Roti Gandum Cokelat**: 123 units
5.  **Roti Bawang Saus Keju**: 121 units
    _Insight: Beverage (Coffee) is the volume leader, suggesting a strong cross-sell opportunity with bakery items._

## 4. Strategic Recommendations (2025 Plan)

Based on the 2024 performance data, the following strategies are recommended to drive growth and normalize revenue distribution:

### 1. Replicate the "June Effect" (Q2 Strategy)

- **Insight**: June generated 59% of annual revenue (Rp28.6M).
- **Action**: Investigate the exact driver of this spike (e.g., specific event, bulk catering, holiday promo).
- **Strategy**: Re-launch this successful campaign in **October/November** to boost Q4 performance, which was the weakest quarter (Rp2.7M).

### 2. Boost Weekday Sales (Friday Focus)

- **Insight**: Friday is the lowest performing day (Avg Rp20,260), while Sunday leads (Rp24,718).
- **Strategy**: Launch a **"Friday Flash Sale"** or "Weekend Starter Pack" promotion.
  - _Example_: "Buy 5 Roti, Get 1 Free Espresso" every Friday to drive volume and clear inventory before the weekend.

### 3. Product Bundling (Coffee + Bakery)

- **Insight**: **Es Kopi Susu** is the #1 volume leader (194 units), followed closely by various Roti items.
- **Strategy**: Creating default combos to increase Average Order Value (AOV).
  - _Bundle Idea_: "Morning Booster" (Es Kopi Susu + Roti Gandum) for a set price.
  - _Bundle Idea_: "Sharing Box" (5x Roti Cranberry variants) for families on Sundays.

### 4. Inventory Management

- **Insight**: H1 (Jan-Jun) contributed 81% of revenue.
- **Strategy**: Adjust inventory procurement to match this seasonality.Reduce waste in H2 (low season) unless marketing interventions (like Strategy #1) are implemented to revive demand.
